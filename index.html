JOSHUA DAVID SCARLETT

Joshua David Scarlett is a New York based actor. He began acting in his early teens after attending John Robert Powers. There, he was scouted by and started freelancing with various talent agencies. Save for one film, Joshua started off exclusively doing commercials and print modeling.

Deciding to receive more intense training, Joshua studied at Marymount Manhattan College before transferring to SUNY Purchase. While at Purchase, he also studied writing, earning him two degrees - one in theatre performance and another in playwriting and screenwriting. During this time, he began writing a feature length film.

Joshua can be seen on television via his co-star roles on I, WITNESS, UNBREAKABLE KIMMY SCHMIDT, HOUSE OF CARDS, and most recently LAW & ORDER: SVU. Theatrically, he has performed in WALK HARD at the Metropolitan Playhouse, A FEW GOOD MEN at The Gallery Players, and Hip to Hip Theatre Co.â€™s repertory productions of MEASURE FOR MEASURE and HENRY IV PART 1.

In addition to his artistic endeavors, Joshua teaches swimming; he was a Division III swimmer for a year while in college. He continues auditioning, working on his film, and finding ways to expand his artistic horizons.
